Wendy Harrison creates unique glasswork inspired by the beautiful countryside and coastline of the Rame Peninsula in south east Cornwall. Her work includes a range includes cards for every occasion, wall hangings, candle holders, glass on canvas and wedding keepsakes. Wendy also welcomes commisions and relishes the opportunity to work with clients to create the perfect gift to make a special occasion.
